Google Ads vs Facebook Ads – Which to Choose?

When it comes to online advertising, Google Ads and Facebook Ads are the two giants. But which one is right for your business?

1. Audience Intent

Google Ads: Targets people actively searching for products or services. Great for high-intent keywords.

Facebook Ads: Targets users based on interests, behavior, and demographics. Great for brand awareness and demand generation.

2. Ad Placement

Google Ads: Shows ads on search results, YouTube, and partner websites.

Facebook Ads: Shows ads on Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and Audience Network.

3. Cost & ROI

Google Ads: Usually higher cost per click, but brings highly targeted traffic ready to convert.

Facebook Ads: Generally lower cost per click, ideal for wider reach and retargeting campaigns.

4. Which to Choose?

If your goal is **direct conversions** and targeting people actively searching, choose Google Ads.
If your goal is **brand awareness, audience building, or retargeting**, choose Facebook Ads.
Ideally, use both platforms strategically for best results.

Final Thoughts

Both platforms have their strengths. Analyse your business goals, budget, and target audience to decide where to invest first.
